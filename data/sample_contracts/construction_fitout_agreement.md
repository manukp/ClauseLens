# OFFICE FIT-OUT AGREEMENT

**This Agreement** is made on 1 August 2025

**BETWEEN** Northwind Offices Ltd ("Client"), of 22 MG Road, Bengaluru 560001

**AND** Stonegate Interiors ("Contractor"), of 5 Industrial Layout, Bengaluru 560058.

## RECITALS

The Client occupies the 12th floor of the premises at 22 MG Road and wishes to procure interior fit-out works. The Contractor has agreed to design and execute those works on the terms set out below.

## 1. DEFINITIONS

1.1 "**Works**" means the interior fit-out of the 12th-floor office described in clause 2.
1.2 "**Practical Completion**" means the stage at which the Works are complete except for minor items that do not prevent occupation.
1.3 "**Variation**" means an addition to, omission from, or change in the Works.

## 2. SCOPE OF WORKS

2.1 The Contractor shall design and execute the interior fit-out of the Client's 12th-floor office, including partitions, ceilings, electrical works, HVAC integration, flooring, and furnishing, in accordance with the approved drawings.

## 3. PROGRAMME

3.1 The Works shall commence on 1 August 2025.

3.2 The Contractor shall achieve Practical Completion within 12 (twelve) weeks of commencement.

## 4. DELIVERABLES AND STANDARDS

4.1 **Partitioning and ceiling works** - completed to the approved drawings and finishes schedule. Acceptance: joint inspection against the finishes schedule.

4.2 **Electrical fit-out** - power and structured data cabling to all workstations, certified to applicable standards. Acceptance: electrical test certificate issued by a licensed electrician.

4.3 **HVAC integration** - connection of workstation zones to the building HVAC, balanced and commissioned.

4.4 **Snagging rectification** - the Contractor shall rectify defects identified following handover.

## 5. CLIENT-SUPPLIED ITEMS AND DEPENDENCIES

5.1 The electrical fit-out (clause 4.2) depends on the Client providing the final power-supply and workstation layout. This layout will be made available to the Contractor.

5.2 The Client shall provide access to the floor and building services during normal working hours.

## 6. PAYMENT AND VARIATIONS

6.1 The contract sum is INR 85,00,000, paid against monthly progress certificates issued by the Client's appointed surveyor.

6.2 Variations to the Works up to INR 5,00,000 in value may be approved and instructed without a formal change order.

6.3 Variations above INR 5,00,000 require a written change order signed by both Parties before the varied work proceeds.

## 7. LIQUIDATED DAMAGES

7.1 If Practical Completion is not achieved by 15 October 2025, liquidated damages of INR 25,000 per day shall apply, up to a cap of 10% of the contract sum.

## 8. INSURANCE

8.1 The Contractor shall maintain contractor's all-risk insurance and public liability insurance of not less than INR 1,00,00,000 for the duration of the Works.

## 9. WARRANTY

9.1 The Contractor warrants the Works against defects in materials and workmanship for 12 months from Practical Completion.

## 10. CONFIDENTIALITY

10.1 Each Party shall keep the other's commercial information confidential.

## 11. TERMINATION

11.1 Either Party may terminate for material breach not cured within 21 days of written notice.

## 12. GOVERNING LAW

12.1 This Agreement is governed by the laws of India, and disputes are subject to arbitration in Bengaluru.
