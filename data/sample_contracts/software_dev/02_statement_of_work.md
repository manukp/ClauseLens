# STATEMENT OF WORK — SOW-01

**Customer Onboarding Platform**

This Statement of Work ("SOW-01") is issued under and governed by the Master Services Agreement dated 1 August 2025 between Acme Corp ("Client") and BuildrCo Pvt Ltd ("Vendor"). Capitalised terms not defined here have the meanings given in the Master Services Agreement. In the event of conflict, the Master Services Agreement prevails unless this SOW expressly states otherwise.

## 1. BACKGROUND AND OBJECTIVES

1.1 The Client requires a customer onboarding platform to replace its existing manual onboarding process. The platform will capture customer records, validate documentation, and integrate with the Client's downstream systems.

1.2 This SOW sets out the Deliverables, acceptance criteria, timeline, and dependencies for the engagement.

## 2. DELIVERABLES

2.1 **Platform Build** — the onboarding web application, comprising the customer registration workflow, document upload, and an administrative console. Acceptance: passes the agreed test plan set out in Appendix A and obtains Client UAT Sign-Off.

2.2 **Data Migration** — migration of existing customer records from the Client's legacy system into the new platform, including data cleansing and validation. Acceptance: a reconciliation report demonstrating 100% record parity, approved by the Client's Data Lead.

2.3 **Performance Tuning** — optimisation of platform response times prior to go-live.

2.4 **UAT Sign-Off** — formal Client acceptance of the platform following user acceptance testing.

2.5 **Hypercare Support** — 30 days of post go-live support, including defect triage and resolution, commencing on the go-live date.

## 3. TIMELINE

3.1 Project start: 1 August 2025.

3.2 Platform Build complete by 15 September 2025.

3.3 UAT Sign-Off to be obtained by 30 September 2025.

3.4 Target go-live: 5 October 2025.

## 4. RESPONSIBILITIES

4.1 The Vendor is responsible for the design, build, and testing of the Platform Build Deliverable (clause 2.1) and for Performance Tuning (clause 2.3).

4.2 The Client is responsible for providing timely access to the legacy system, nominating UAT participants, and providing test data by 20 August 2025.

## 5. DEPENDENCIES

5.1 Data Migration (clause 2.2) is a prerequisite for UAT and must be completed before UAT can begin. Data Migration is scheduled to complete by 15 October 2025.

5.2 The Platform Build depends on the Client confirming the final data schema by 25 August 2025.

## 6. ACCEPTANCE PROCEDURE

6.1 On submission of a Deliverable, the Client has 10 business days to review and either accept it or provide written notice of non-conformities.

6.2 If the Client does not respond within the review period, the Deliverable is deemed accepted.

6.3 The Vendor shall remedy notified non-conformities within 10 business days and resubmit for review.
