# CHANGE ORDER — CO-01

**Amendment to SOW-01 (Customer Onboarding Platform)**

This Change Order ("CO-01") is issued under clause 5 of the Master Services Agreement dated 1 August 2025 and amends Statement of Work SOW-01 between Acme Corp ("Client") and BuildrCo Pvt Ltd ("Vendor"). Capitalised terms have the meanings given in the Master Services Agreement and SOW-01. Except as amended here, SOW-01 remains in full force.

**Date of this Change Order:** 5 September 2025

## 1. REASON FOR CHANGE

1.1 Following a strategic review, the Client wishes to (a) accelerate go-live to capture an upcoming campaign window, and (b) add a third-party CRM integration to the scope.

## 2. CHANGES TO SCOPE

2.1 A new Deliverable is added to SOW-01:

**2.1.1 CRM Integration** — a real-time integration between the onboarding platform and the Client's Salesforce CRM, including field mapping and error handling. Acceptance: passes an integration test demonstrating successful record sync across all mapped fields.

## 3. CHANGES TO TIMELINE

3.1 The target go-live date in clause 3.4 of SOW-01 is brought forward from 5 October 2025 to **25 September 2025**.

3.2 All other dates in SOW-01, including the Data Migration completion date, remain unchanged unless expressly amended here.

## 4. CHANGES TO FEES

4.1 The additional fee for the CRM Integration Deliverable is INR 8,00,000, payable on acceptance of that Deliverable.

4.2 This additional fee is added to the total contract value, bringing it to INR 56,00,000.

## 5. EFFECT

5.1 This Change Order, once signed by both Parties, forms part of SOW-01 and the Master Services Agreement.
