# MARKETING SERVICES RETAINER AGREEMENT

**This Agreement** is made on 1 September 2025

**BETWEEN** Lumio Brands ("Client"), of 9 Brigade Road, Bengaluru 560025

**AND** Vermillion Studio ("Agency"), of 31 Indiranagar, Bengaluru 560038.

## RECITALS

The Client wishes to engage the Agency to plan and execute its digital marketing campaigns on a retainer basis. The Agency has agreed to provide such services on the terms below.

## 1. DEFINITIONS

1.1 "**Services**" means the marketing services described in clause 2.
1.2 "**Deliverables**" means the materials and reports produced under clause 3.
1.3 "**Media Spend**" means amounts committed to third-party advertising platforms on the Client's behalf.

## 2. SERVICES

2.1 The Agency shall plan and run paid social and search campaigns, produce creative assets, manage Media Spend, and report on campaign performance.

2.2 The Agency shall assign a dedicated account manager as the Client's primary contact.

## 3. DELIVERABLES

3.1 **Creative assets** - campaign imagery, copy, and video produced for each monthly push, delivered in agreed formats. Acceptance: Client review and written approval within 5 business days.

3.2 **Monthly campaign report** - a report summarising campaign performance for each calendar month.

3.3 **Quarterly strategy review** - a working session reviewing results and agreeing the next quarter's plan. Acceptance: documented minutes signed off by both Parties.

## 4. MEDIA BUDGET

4.1 The Agency may commit Media Spend of up to INR 10,00,000 per month on the Client's behalf.

4.2 The Agency shall report actual Media Spend against budget in each monthly report.

## 5. CLIENT DATA

5.1 The Client will share customer contact lists and audience segments with the Agency for campaign targeting and audience matching.

5.2 The Agency shall use such data solely for the purposes of providing the Services.

## 6. FEES

6.1 The retainer fee is INR 4,00,000 per month, invoiced monthly in advance. Payment authorisation: the Client's Marketing Director.

6.2 Media Spend is invoiced separately at cost.

6.3 Invoices are payable within 15 days of receipt.

## 7. TERM AND RENEWAL

7.1 This Agreement runs for an initial term of six months, commencing on 1 September 2025 and ending on 31 December 2025.

7.2 Unless either Party gives notice, this Agreement renews automatically on an annual basis.

## 8. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

8.1 Each Party retains ownership of intellectual property it owned before this Agreement.

8.2 The Agency grants the Client a licence to use the campaign materials for the duration of the Agreement.

## 9. CONFIDENTIALITY

9.1 Each Party shall keep the other's Confidential Information secret during the term and for two years thereafter.

## 10. TERMINATION

10.1 Either Party may terminate for material breach not cured within 30 days of written notice.

## 11. GOVERNING LAW

11.1 This Agreement is governed by the laws of India, and disputes are subject to arbitration in Bengaluru.
