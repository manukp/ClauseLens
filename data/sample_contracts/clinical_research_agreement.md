# CLINICAL RESEARCH SERVICES AGREEMENT

**This Agreement** is made on 1 September 2025

**BETWEEN** Meridian Health ("Sponsor"), of 100 Health City, Bengaluru 560066

**AND** Helix Labs ("CRO"), of 48 Biotech Park, Bengaluru 560100.

## RECITALS

The Sponsor wishes to engage the CRO to conduct a Phase II clinical study (the "Study"). The CRO has the regulatory expertise and site network to conduct the Study and has agreed to do so on the terms below.

## 1. DEFINITIONS

1.1 "**Study**" means the Phase II clinical study described in the Protocol.
1.2 "**Protocol**" means the study protocol agreed by the Parties and approved by the relevant ethics committee.
1.3 "**Investigational Product**" means the drug product under investigation in the Study.
1.4 "**Pass-Through Costs**" means third-party costs incurred by the CRO in conducting the Study.

## 2. SCOPE OF SERVICES

2.1 The CRO shall conduct the Study, including site identification and management, patient enrolment, data collection, monitoring, and statistical analysis, in accordance with the Protocol and applicable regulations.

## 3. DELIVERABLES

3.1 **Site initiation** - activation of all trial sites, evidenced by initiation visit reports.

3.2 **Interim analysis report** - an analysis of trial data at the midpoint of the Study.

3.3 **Final study report** - the complete clinical study report. Acceptance: review and written approval by the Sponsor's Medical Director.

## 4. TIMELINE

4.1 Patient enrolment will be completed by the end of Q3 2025.

4.2 The interim analysis report (clause 3.2) is due by the end of Q2 2025.

4.3 The final study report is due within 90 days of database lock.

## 5. SPONSOR OBLIGATIONS

5.1 The Sponsor will supply the Investigational Product for distribution to the trial sites.

5.2 The Sponsor will provide the approved Protocol and any reference materials needed for the Study.

## 6. BUDGET AND PAYMENT

6.1 The service fee is INR 1,20,00,000, invoiced against milestones set out in the budget schedule.

6.2 Pass-Through Costs (site fees, laboratory tests, logistics) will be reimbursed as incurred.

6.3 Invoices are payable within 45 days of receipt.

## 7. CONFIDENTIALITY

7.1 The CRO shall keep all Study information confidential and shall not publish results without the Sponsor's prior written consent.

## 8. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

8.1 All data and results arising from the Study are the property of the Sponsor.

## 9. TERMINATION

9.1 The Sponsor may terminate this Agreement on 60 days' written notice. On termination, the CRO is paid for Services performed and non-cancellable commitments incurred up to the termination date.

## 10. GOVERNING LAW

10.1 This Agreement is governed by the laws of India, and disputes are subject to arbitration in Bengaluru.
